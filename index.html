About.zs

Zach, like many dev bootcamp students, has a diverse and non-linear career history. Zach has had a lifelong interest in studing systems and logical interactions, and has finally made the commitment to jump full time into studying development.

After dropping out of his first attempt to get a degree in hard sciences, Zach found himself working in the service industry. He quickly discovered a knack for working with people. Over the next few years, he finished a B.S. in Economics and climbed the management ladder working in retail operations. His interest in behavioral and business systems led him to transition to work in Human Resources. He grew to manage HR for a retail unit with 200+ staff and eventually left as an HR business partner for a small business consultancy.

Although he loved the systems-thinking necessary for strategic HR work, Zach wanted to work in a field that was more open to creativity and exploration. He chose web development because it lives on the border between technical and behavioral systems and allows interesing and creative work with both.

Zach is a 4th genreation Portland native and has lived here almost his whole life. He lives in north portland with his wife, two cats, and some chickens. You can find him browsing in the Kenton library most weekends.
